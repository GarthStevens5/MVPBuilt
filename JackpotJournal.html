Jackpot Journal

Debuting in the January 2015, Jackpot Journal is our flagship publication and the first entertainment magazine of its kind. Unlike most magazines which rely heavily on ads, Jackpot Journal dedicates a bulk of its content solely to you, the reader. Throughout its pages, you find hundreds of thousands of dollars in cash and prize opportunities that you can enter to win. As part of our service, we provide details on what you can win, how to enter, what deadlines must be meet, and who’s responsible for choosing the winner of each prize. In addition, each issue comes interesting and educational articles, challenging puzzles and games, tasty recipes, and exciting DIY projects.
