Mountain View Publishers

Businesses in virtually every industry use sweepstakes as an effective marketing tool to build brand awareness, generate excitement and incentivize people to shop with them. This has given sweepstakes prize enthusiasts, known also as “sweepers,” a wealth of opportunities to take advantage of. Many sweepers have even made careers out of entering and winning as many sweepstakes as they can possibly find.

With so many promotional offers flooding the market, it can be difficult to find what you’re looking for. Where do you look? What can you win? How do you enter? When is the deadline? Who chooses the winners? These are all very important questions, and you can spend endless hours of your time trying to find an answer. This, of course, requires a lot of patience and persistence, something most of us don’t have in infinite supply.

Mountain View Publishers (MVP) decided to create a permanent solution for this problem. MVP provides a unique service by researching, verifying and compiling the most appealing prize opportunities available to the public into a convenient, entertaining, and easy-to- follow prize guide. Each month our subscribers enjoy a publication loaded with informative articles, challenging puzzles and games, recipes, DIY projects, as well as complete information on how to enter and win hundreds of thousands of dollars in cash and prizes.

With each opportunity, we carefully detail all the entry instructions you will need, what deadlines you must meet, where to send your entry (if applicable), and, of course, how much you can win. By doing this, we save you time, money and frustration. No more combing through newspapers or staring at computer screens all day. No more wasted postage or second guessing. With our products and services, you can find all the prizes you’re looking for, save your precious time and resources, and, best of all, have fun doing it.
